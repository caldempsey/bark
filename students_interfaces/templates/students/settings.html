What are learning styles?

Learning styles are a nifty way of saying how you best learn based on cognitive functions. That's a fancy way of saying, this is the best way you learn!

The goal of Bark is to tailor lesson content to those preferences. In particular, we aim to best facilitate student learning of Java (but our platform is not limited to that).
